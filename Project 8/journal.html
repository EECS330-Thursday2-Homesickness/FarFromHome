My Journal

I'm really excited for Thanksgiving break! I will be going home to Pittsburgh and spending time with my family. I will also be able to see all of my friends from high school. Every year on Thansgiving, we have a big thanksgiving dinner and go around in a circle talking about what we are grateful for. This year, I'm grateful that I was able to experience my first semester of college and meet all my new friends. Even though I still feel homesick, I feel better knowing that I can go home soon.
